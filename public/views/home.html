ABOUT ARIDE


Aride is a long distance ridesharing service, connecting drivers with empty seats to people travelling the same way. With Aride, you can find Notre Dame friends, classmates, and coworkers going the same way you are.

Have a car? Need a ride?


Aride helps you offer or request rides for commutes, road trips, and popular events. If you have a car, split costs by offering rides. If you don't have a car, find rides where you need to go.

A pleasant ride-sharing experience


Using personal network analysis algorithm, Aride matches you with people with common friends and similar interests to guarantee you a pleasant ride.
